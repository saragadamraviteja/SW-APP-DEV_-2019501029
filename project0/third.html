Araku













One of my favourite place is Araku. what a cool climate. In my college days i used to go araku for drinking coffee.

Araku is famous for coffee plants,pepper and the climate. It is a hill station which is surrounded with greenery.

I never feel bore to visit araku because of the place like BURRA CAVES, CHAPARAYA, PADERU, KOLLAPUT RESORT, KATIKA WATERFALLS...
